## 🏠 Accommodation Guide (Backpacker)

### Bangkok — $8-15/night
- **NapPark Hostel** — Khaosan area, social, rooftop bar, $12/night dorm
- **Lub d Silom** — Cleaner, quieter, $15/night, near BTS
- ⚠️ Avoid: Khaosan Road party hostels if you need sleep

### Chiang Mai — $6-12/night
- **Stamps Backpackers** — Old City, amazing common area, $8/night
- **Hug Hostel** — Near Night Bazaar, free breakfast, $10/night

### Luang Prabang — $8-15/night
- **Dreamtime Hostel** — Central, river views, $10/night
- ⚠️ Laos is more expensive than Thailand for hostels

### Hanoi — $6-12/night
- **Hanoi Old Quarter Hostel** — Perfect location, $8/night
- **Nexy Hostel** — Social, pub crawls, rooftop, $10/night

**Key phrase**: "กินเจ" (gin jay) = "I eat vegetarian"

### Hanoi
- **"Chay"** restaurants = vegetarian (Buddhist)
- Phở chay (vegetarian phở) — $1-2 everywhere
- **Loving Hut** chain — reliable vegan, $2-3/meal

## 🎒 Top Budget Activities

### Bangkok (Free - $15)
1. **Grand Palace + Wat Pho** — $15 entry, non-negotiable must-see
2. **Chatuchak Weekend Market** — Free entry, biggest market in the world
3. **Lumpini Park** — Free, monitor lizards, morning tai chi
4. **Chinatown (Yaowarat)** — Free to walk, incredible street food
5. **Temple hopping** — Most temples free or $1-2

### Chiang Mai (Free - $20)
1. **Doi Suthep Temple** — $1 entry + $2 songthaew ride up
2. **Monk Chat** — Free! Chat with monks practicing English at Wat Chedi Luang
3. **Sunday Walking Street Market** — Free, incredible handmade goods
4. **Cooking Class** — $15-25, learn to cook Thai food (4-5 dishes!)
5. **Ethical Elephant Sanctuary** — $40-60 (splurge but worth it, research carefully)

### Luang Prabang
1. **Alms Giving Ceremony** — Free to watch (5:30 AM, be respectful!)
2. **Kuang Si Waterfalls** — $3 entry + $3 tuk-tuk share
3. **Night Market** — Free to browse, amazing textiles
4. **Mekong River sunset** — Free from any riverbank

### Hanoi
1. **Old Quarter walking** — Free, beautifully chaotic
2. **Hoan Kiem Lake** — Free, peaceful morning walks
3. **Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum** — Free (closed Mondays & Fridays)
4. **Egg Coffee** — $1.50, uniquely Hanoi experience (try Cafe Giang)
5. **Train Street** — Free, watch trains pass inches from cafes

## 🛡️ SE Asia Backpacker Safety

### Top Scams to Watch
1. 🔴 **Motorbike rental damage scam** — Photo everything, NEVER leave passport
2. 🔴 **Gem store scam (Bangkok)** — NO gems are "investment grade"
3. 🟡 **Tuk-tuk "tour" detours** — They'll take you to commission shops
4. 🟡 **Fake travel agencies** — Book direct or use Hostelworld/12Go Asia
5. 🟡 **Full Moon Party theft** — Don't bring valuables to beach parties

### Vegetarian Safety
- "No fish sauce" is as important as "no meat" — say "ไม่ใส่น้ำปลา" (mai sai nam pla)
- "Jay" (เจ) = strict vegan (no garlic/onion too, Buddhist)
- "Mangsawirat" (มังสวิรัติ) = vegetarian (more relaxed)
