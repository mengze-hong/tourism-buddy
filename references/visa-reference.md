# Visa Quick Reference

Common visa-free / visa-on-arrival / e-visa information for popular destinations.

⚠️ **ALWAYS search for the latest info** — visa rules change frequently!

## Visa-Free Destinations (for many nationalities)

### 30-90 Day Visa-Free (varies by passport)
- 🇹🇭 Thailand — 30 days (extendable to 60 at immigration office)
- 🇯🇵 Japan — 90 days (no extension, no work)
- 🇰🇷 South Korea — 90 days (K-ETA may be required)
- 🇸🇬 Singapore — 30 days
- 🇲🇾 Malaysia — 90 days
- 🇮🇩 Indonesia — 30 days free / 30 days VOA ($35, extendable)
- 🇵🇭 Philippines — 30 days
- 🇭🇰 Hong Kong — 90 days
- 🇲🇴 Macau — 30 days
- 🇹🇼 Taiwan — 90 days (for many Western passports)

### Europe (Schengen Area)
- 90 days within any 180-day period
- Covers 27 countries (most of EU + Iceland, Norway, Switzerland)
- **ETIAS required** starting 2025 for visa-exempt nationals (US, UK, etc.)
- ⚠️ Count carefully — 90/180 rule applies to ALL Schengen combined

### Americas
- 🇺🇸 USA — ESTA for VWP countries (90 days)
- 🇨🇦 Canada — eTA for visa-exempt countries
- 🇲🇽 Mexico — 180 days for many nationalities
- 🇧🇷 Brazil — e-visa for US/Canada/Australia/Japan citizens
- 🇦🇷 Argentina — 90 days for most Western passports
- 🇨🇴 Colombia — 90 days for most nationalities

## E-Visa Destinations
- 🇮🇳 India — e-visa (30/90/365 day options, apply 4 days+ before)
- 🇹🇷 Turkey — e-visa ($50 for US citizens, varies)
- 🇰🇭 Cambodia — e-visa ($36 + $6 service fee)
- 🇻🇳 Vietnam — e-visa (30 days, $25)
- 🇱🇰 Sri Lanka — ETA (30 days, ~$50)
- 🇪🇹 Ethiopia — e-visa ($82 for 30 days)
- 🇦🇺 Australia — ETA or eVisitor (free-$20)
- 🇳🇿 New Zealand — NZeTA ($12-17)

## Visa-on-Arrival
- 🇪🇬 Egypt — VOA ($25 single entry)
- 🇯🇴 Jordan — VOA (free with Jordan Pass purchase)
- 🇳🇵 Nepal — VOA ($30/15 days, $50/30 days)
- 🇱🇦 Laos — VOA ($30-42 depending on nationality)
- 🇲🇬 Madagascar — VOA (free for 30 days)

## Important Reminders
1. **6-Month Passport Rule**: Many countries require passport valid for 6+ months beyond entry date
2. **Blank Pages**: Some countries require 1-2 blank pages
3. **Proof of Onward Travel**: Many countries can ask for this — book a cheap refundable ticket
4. **Proof of Funds**: Some countries check you have sufficient money
5. **Travel Insurance**: Increasingly required (especially post-COVID)
6. **Yellow Fever Certificate**: Required if arriving from endemic countries (Africa, South America)
7. **Israeli Stamp Issue**: Some Middle Eastern countries deny entry with Israeli stamps (Israel now uses entry cards, not stamps)

## Useful Tools
- **Passport Index**: passportindex.org — check visa requirements by nationality
- **Sherpa**: joinsherpa.com — travel restriction checker
- **iVisa**: ivisa.com — visa application service (charges a fee)
- **IATA Travel Centre**: iata.org — official airline travel document requirements
